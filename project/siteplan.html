Smart Campus Companion – Site Plan

Site Name

Smart Campus Companion

This name was chosen because the website acts as a digital companion for students, helping them navigate campus life more efficiently through planning and organization tools.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Smart Campus Companion website is to help students easily discover study locations on campus, organize their weekly schedules, and save personal preferences using interactive and dynamic web technologies.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a quiet study spot on campus between my classes?
  • How can I organize my weekly class schedule and personal activities in place?

Pages

  • Home
  • Study Spots
  • Weekly Planner
  • References

Color Palette

  • Primary Color: #2c3e50 (Dark Blue) - Used for headings and header/footer backgrounds
  • Secondary Color: #3498db (Blue) - Used for accents, links and section highlights
  • Accent Color: #ecf0f1 (Light Gray) - Used for page backgroun and contrast

Typography

  • Playfair Display: Used for all headings
  • Roboto: Used for body text and lists
