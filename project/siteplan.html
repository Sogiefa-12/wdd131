Smart Campus Companion – Site Plan

Site Name

Smart Campus Companion

This name represents a helpful tool designed to support students in managing campus life.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Smart Campus Companion website is to help students easily discover study locations on campus, organize their weekly schedules, and save personal preferences using interactive and dynamic web technologies.

Target Audience

The target audience is college students who want a simple, organized way to plan their campus activities and find suitable study environments.

Pages

  • Home
  • Study Spots
  • Weekly Planner
  • References

Color Palette

  • Secondary Color: #3498db (Blue)
  • Accent Color: #ecf0f1 (Light Gray)

Typography
